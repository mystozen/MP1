About MoonStar Builders Inc.

Our Story

Moonstar Builders Inc. Started in 2013 and was incorporated in 2016. Specialized in sports flooring construction.

One of the leading Sports Flooring Contractor in Manila and all over the Philippines.

Expanded into general construction building how to mid-rise structures in the entire Philippines.

Servicing Government Entities, Religious Institutions, Private Companies, Individuals and Institutions like Schools, Sports Associations, Leisure Clubs and more.

Mission

To go beyond our customers’ satisfaction and expectations by providing the best services and solutions to their general and sports construction needs.

Vision

To be the leader in the general and sports construction industry, and a key contributor to the nation’s urban and social development through quality products and efficient services.

ISO CERTIFICATES

Moonstar Builders Inc. is a certified ISO 9001:2015, ISO 15062:2014 and ISO 67023:2016 company.
